PARIS IS BURNING

For a month only, Prince Charles Cinema will showcase the original remastered documentary that brought us drag, ball culture and Opulence.

After 29 years "Paris is burning is back to bring the "Eleganza and Extravaganza" back into the world

A chronicle of New York's drag scene in the 1980s, focusing on balls, voguing and the ambitions and dreams of those who gave the era its warmth and vitality.

The movie has inspired generations to be their true selfs and embrace their uniqueness. A forefront of LGBTQ stories, you will have a chance to learn the history and relive the excite that pushe the change to the whole world.
