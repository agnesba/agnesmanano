Introduction

Kim Nam-joon (Hangul: 김남준) better known as RM

He is the leader and main rapper of the South Korean boy group BTS, and made his debut on June 13, 2013, as a member of BTS on Mnet's M! Countdown with the track "No More Dream" from their debut single album '2 Cool 4 Skool'. Together with the members of BTS, he went on to win a series of awards and performed at several events such as the Mnet Asian Music Awards (MAMA).

As of December 2017, he has an estimated net worth of $8 million.

On Oct 23, 2018, he released a second mixtape/playlist titled 'Mono'.
